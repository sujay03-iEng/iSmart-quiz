Welcome to the official page of the iSmart Quiz Competition – an annual event proudly conducted by iEngineering Group for the past five years. This competition is designed to challenge, engage, and celebrate the brightest engineering minds across Oceania, Australia, and Asia-Pacific regions.

🧠 What is the iSmart Quiz

The iSmart Quiz is an exciting and highly educational competition that tests students' knowledge in a range of Engineering and Science disciplines. It aims to encourage academic excellence, critical thinking, and international participation in a friendly yet competitive environment.
